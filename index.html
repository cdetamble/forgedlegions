Upcoming multiplayer RTS/RPG mix for PC.

Command a party of stranded medieval warriors on a remote island to find battle, treasury, death or glory.

Features

  • Team-Based Multiplayer with up to 15 players
  • Real-Time Strategy Worldmap with Base Building
  • Turn-based Combat on Hexagon Fields
  • Hire and train up to 8 Medieval Warriors (Female & Male)
  • ~50 collectible Weapons, Helmets, Shields and Body Armor (3 Tiers each)
  • Orchestral Soundtrack
  • and more...

About

Developed by a single developer using LWJGL, the game is inspired by Battle Brothers and Warcraft 3.

It aims to combine the deadly tactical combat and unit progression of BB with the real-time territory fights of WC3.
